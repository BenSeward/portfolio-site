Ben Seward

Hi, I'm Ben & I am a Front-End Designer from England.

My name is Ben Seward. I am a multi disciplinary designer/front-end developer based in Bath, UK. I have over 5 years professional experience predominantly within the e-commerce sector designing beautiful interfaces, websites and marketing pieces for companies of varying sizes. Currently I am working as an e-commerce manager whilst also producing regular freelance work.

My experience started with simple web design, quickly moving onto Wordpress catalogue sites and then onto Magento which is the e-commerce platform I have been developing on for some time now. If you're a small business looking for a web presence email me at ben.seward@googlemail.com and I'm happy to help.
